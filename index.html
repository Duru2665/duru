British Academy

Welcome to Dreamboat British Academy, tradition meets innovation
in education! Our dynamic curriculum blends the best of British and Nigerian
educational standards with the innovative principles of Montessori
education, offering a comprehensive and enriching learning experience for
young minds.

Learning Begins
With Us

We, at Dreamboat Academy offer supportive and inspirational
environments for young enquiring minds to learn and grow with us. Our
passion for learning means we achieve more than outstanding results. We
strive to build confident and creative thinkers and aim at delivering an
education that is truly relevant to their future.

____

We are an early learning academy focused on social-emotional
development and early literacy and numeracy. Our students walk out with
the character and confidence to make their mark in the world, equipped
with the knowledge and real-world skills that take them way ahead in the
industry they may serve.
